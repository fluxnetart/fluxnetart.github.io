Meet the artists and join us at the Patricia Valian Reser Center for Creative Arts (PRAx) on 22 January, 2026 for the opening celebration of a special art-science showcase featuring artworks from the 2024-25 <i>flux</i>ART cohort. <a href="https://prax.oregonstate.edu/events/opening-celebration-currents-experiments-art-science-collaboration">Event info and ticketing</a>

Come experience ecosystem fluxes and reimagine the infinite interconnections that sustain life on Earth.
Together we will explore how artistic inquiry can transform flux science and how scientific curiosity fosters artistic creativity.
